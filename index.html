    Senses

Bio

Thieving Motives is a force born from chaos, emotion, and defiance. With gripping lyricism and raw intensity, their music captures the struggles of existence—blurring the line between fear and resistance.

Their latest single, Sleep Syndrome, is a frantic escape through paranoia and self-destruction, where suffocating terror collides with the desperate need to break free. Pulsing with urgency, the track spirals through haunting reflections and distorted reality, leaving nothing but adrenaline in its wake.

Senses flips the narrative—calling out the illusion of freedom and the systems built to control. A rallying cry for those refusing to be shackled, it surges with explosive energy, demanding rebellion in a world that wants submission.
